O

OBJECTIVE

What is
this?

To let you experience a world where reality depends on observation. The objects, sounds, and text in this universe exist, change, or disappear based on your attention.

01

SCIENCE

The observed
particle.

The project is inspired by quantum mechanics. Particles behave differently when observed. Here, objects follow similar rules. When you look at them, they appear or change.

"The act of measurement changes what is being measured."
02

ART

The living
canvas.

The visuals, typography, and motion are carefully designed. The environment is an interactive artwork where every element is part of the composition and reacts to your presence.

03

PHILOSOPHY

Does it exist
if unseen?

The experience raises questions about reality. Does something exist if it is not seen? Your interaction shapes what is real in this universe.

04

TECHNOLOGY

Your unique
universe.

The system uses generative code to make objects, sounds, and text respond to your attention. Each session is unique. You create your own version of this universe by interacting.

05

SPIRITUALITY

Your attention
creates.

The artwork reflects impermanence and awareness. Observing the environment is an act of participation. Your attention creates the universe around you.

06

HOW TO USE THE UNIVERSE

What to
expect.

◎

OBSERVE

Move your cursor. Objects materialise only when you look at them. Stop — watch them dissolve back into uncertainty.

◈

COLLAPSE

Click anywhere to force a quantum collapse. Nearby objects blink into or out of existence. Every click is unrepeatable.

◇

LISTEN

Sound responds to your gaze. Each particle carries a tone. Move slowly — the universe sings differently for each observer.

○

REFLECT

There is no goal. No score. Only presence. Ask yourself — what exists in the dark between your observations?
